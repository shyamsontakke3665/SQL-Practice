 20] WAQTD DETAILS OF EMP ALONG WITH ANNUAL SALARY IF THE ANNUAL SALARY IS MORE THAN RS.15000 IN DEPTNO 10 OR 
20 EXCEPT EMP'S HIRED IN THE YEAR 82.
